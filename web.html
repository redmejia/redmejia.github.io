Win pay

Win pay is a payment processor this is a project where I wanted to practice microservices using GO. Win project has four services. The controller service works as an intermediate between the front-end and the other services. Authorization service that handles the login of a company or a registration of a new company. The invoice service handles purchase information accepted or declined transactions. Fake cards service this service works as a bank where a transaction is verified returning trasaction codes of 02 accepted or 04 for declined.
